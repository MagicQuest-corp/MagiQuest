ABOUT MagiQuest
Welcome to MagiQuest!

At Magiquest, we believe that a lively and dynamic college experience is shaped by the active interaction and the power of community. Our innovative quest system encourages college learners to participate actively in acmpus life by providing enjoyable, fulfilling, and meaningful experiences.


How it Works

Students can participate in a variety of quests, including helping campus clubs recruit members, organizing study sessions, and engaging in community service projects like campus clean-ups. Completing these quests earns points that can be exchanged for exclusive rewards.


Our unique currency system, MagiPoints, rewards your efforts and contributions, which you can use to buy coupons for discounts on snacks, food items in the cafeteria, and university merchandise, as well as unlock exclusive offers. Additionally, MagiQuest features a built-in messaging system to help you connect with fellow students, making it easy to coordinate study sessions, discuss quests, or just have a chat.
